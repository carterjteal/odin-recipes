Description

Pepperoni is an American variety of spicy salami made from cured pork and beef seasoned with paprika or other chili pepper.

Prior to cooking, pepperoni is characteristically soft, slightly smoky, and bright red.[1] Thinly sliced pepperoni is one of the most popular pizza toppings in American pizzerias.
